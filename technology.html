The Problem

Infertility affects 15% of couples in the U.S., with 10 million women affected. Access to diagnostic tools like the HSG is limited due to rural constraints and referral requirements. Current procedures expose patients to radiation and require specialist facilities.

Our Solution: FertChx TSDS

The FertChx TSDS offers a simple, office-based alternative with no radiation exposure and no need for specialist referrals. This innovative system is cost-effective and enhances accessibility for underserved patients.
