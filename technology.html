The Problem

Infertility affects 15% of couples in the U.S., with over 10 million women impacted. Access to diagnostic tools like the HSG is limited due to rural constraints and referral requirements. Current procedures expose patients to radiation and require specialist facilities.

Standard HSG Exam (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Dye injection during HSG
Step 2: X-ray imaging and vascular uptake detection

Our Solution: FertChx TSDS

The FertChx TSDS offers a simple, office-based alternative with no radiation exposure and no need for specialist referrals. This innovative system is cost-effective and enhances accessibility for underserved patients.

FertChx TSDS Procedure (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Placement of TSDS foam device
Step 2: Foam conforming to uterine cavity for evaluation
